How It Started

Built From a Love of Small Moments

We started Wicked Wax because we wanted something that didn't exist yet — a place where you could choose every element of your candle, from the wax colour to the scent to the jar, and make something that felt completely yours.

Every wax colour was chosen for a feeling. Every fragrance oil was blended for a moment. Every jar was picked because it deserved to hold something special. Nothing here is an afterthought.

— Made with care, for moments worth keeping.
